Ontology & Taxonomy

Define brand aliases, category taxonomy, and attribute vocabularies to harmonize data across sources.

Designed to integrate JSON/RDF ontologies and rule sets.

  • RDF/OWL: Classes and properties to define products, brands, categories, and attributes with constraints and inheritance.
  • SHACL: Shape constraints to validate required fields, value ranges, and unit patterns for product specifications.
  • SKOS: Controlled vocabularies for category hierarchies and brand aliases with preferred and alternate labels.
  • SPARQL: Query rules for lookups, alias resolution, and reconciliation during cleaning and reporting.

Usage: map raw values to canonical labels, enforce shapes for completeness, detect conflicts, and generate consistent outputs.
