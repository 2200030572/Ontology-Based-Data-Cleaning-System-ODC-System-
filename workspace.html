Ontology & Taxonomy

RDF/OWL for schema, SHACL for shapes, SKOS for vocabularies, and SPARQL for reconciliation.

RDF/OWL
  • Define Product, Brand, Category classes and semantic properties.
  • Use domains/ranges and subclassing to model inheritance.
SHACL
  • Enforce required fields, value types, and unit patterns.
  • Validate category membership and attribute ranges.
SKOS
  • Maintain taxonomy with broader/narrower relations.
  • Manage brand aliases with prefLabel/altLabel.
SPARQL
  • Resolve aliases to canonical IRIs.
  • Fetch ancestors and detect duplicates.
