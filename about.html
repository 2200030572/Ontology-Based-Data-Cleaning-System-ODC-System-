About ODC System

Driving product data quality with an ontology-first approach.

View Features
Mission
Ontology-first

Help merchants unify noisy catalogs from multiple sources, cutting duplicate SKUs and inconsistent attributes.

Approach
  • RDF/OWL for semantic schema of products, brands, categories, and attributes.
  • SHACL for shape validation of required fields, value types, and units.
  • SKOS for controlled vocabularies and taxonomy with aliases.
  • SPARQL for lookups, reconciliation, and reporting queries.
Outcomes
  • Cleaner search and navigation via canonical categories and brands.
  • Reliable analytics from standardized attributes and units.
  • Faster onboarding of new feeds with repeatable validations.
